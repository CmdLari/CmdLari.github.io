About the studio

Developer · Designer · Illustrator · Monster NPC Whisperer

Hi, I’m Larissa Pychlau — the developer, designer, and illustrator behind LAPYS, a solo studio crafting cozy, character-driven games with a handmade charm and a little chaos under the hood.
With a background in software engineering and visual design, I build almost every part of my games — from logic systems and pixel art to narrative and UI — infusing each world with warmth, intention, and a love for the strange and memorable.
