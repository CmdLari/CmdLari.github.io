Tenfold Tales

a hundred tiles. a dozen choices. one little bunny’s fate.

A cozy oldschool adventure where you explore a magical map, one tile at a time.

Guide Lapin, a curious bunny girl, through a patchwork world of strange events, tough decisions, and stat-driven surprises. With every new board, you’ll face forks in the path—and the outcomes are anything but predictable.

Will you thrive on luck, logic, or sheer bunny stubbornness? Each game is a self-contained journey, full of colour, stats-based gambles, and little stories. Play again and again to uncover new tiles, write new stories, and let the bunny girl explore more.
