ORPHEUS' REVENGE

he came back for love. he stayed for vengeance.

death isn’t the end — and you’re armed with grief, wrath, and hopefully good aim.

In this fast-paced, 80s-infused pixel shooter, you descend once more into the underworld to challenge its lords, beasts, and demons. Fight your way through vibrant hellscapes, accompanied by retro synths, biting dialogue, and enemies that hit harder than your grief.

Click to shoot. Hope to survive.

Features

Pixel Art & Neon: An 80s-style arcade underworld. Expect vibrant backdrops, cursed palettes, and synthwave beats.
Click-to-Kill Mayhem: No cooldowns, no reloads — just click your way through spirits and spiteful bosses.
Trash-Talking Bosses: Why get roasted online when pixelated deities can insult your aim and your life choices?
High Score: No saves, just skill. Track your best runs and try not to rage quit.
